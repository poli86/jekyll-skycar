PAINT CORRECTION

Vehicle Decontamination:

Outfit your vehicle with a variety of customized clear bra kits, tailored for the body shape & designed specifically for covering every inch of the paint. From the most basic, entry level paint protection, to completely custom total car coverage, we offer more combinations of clear bra coverage than anywhere else. Want custom tailored pieces for specific coverage? We do that too.

1 Stage Paint Enhancemen

Starting @$199

We start with a full decontamination of your vehicles exterior. We then machine apply a premium All-in-One Polish to remove swirl marks, hazing, and restore a deep gloss. Lastly a silica paint sealant is applied and buffed to a beautiful finish with a premium microfiber towel.

2 Stage Cut & Polish

Starting @$449

We start with a full decontamination of your vehicles exterior. We then machine apply a Light Cut Compound to remove water etching, swirl marks and scratches. We follow up with a Finishing Polish to restore a deep gloss. Lastly a silica paint sealant is applied and buffed to a beautiful finish with a premium microfiber towel.

3 Stage Heavy Cut & Polish

Starting @$799

We start with a full decontamination of your vehicles exterior. We then machine apply a Heavy Cut Compound to remove most water etching, swirl marks and scratches. Next comes a Heavy Polish as an intermediate step to remove all holograms and buffer trails. We follow up with a Finishing Polish to restore a deep gloss. Lastly a silica paint sealant is applied and buffed to a beautiful finish with a premium microfiber towel.
